Wings of Fire

An Autobiography of Abdul Kalam

by Arun Tiwari

About This Book

Wings of Fire is an autobiography of APJ Abdul Kalam, former President of India. It was written by Dr. Kalam and Arun Tiwari. The book traces the life of Kalam from his childhood to his presidential role, covering his early life, his work on the Indian space program and his role in the development of India's missile systems. The book provides a powerful message about the power of determination, hard work and resilience in achieving one's dreams.

The 3 Mistakes of My Life

A novel by Chetan Bhagat

by Chetan Bhagat

About This Book

The 3 Mistakes of My Life is the story of three friends - Govind, Omi, and Ishaan - who open a cricket shop in Ahmedabad. The novel explores their dreams, struggles, and the three mistakes that change their lives forever. Set against the backdrop of Gujarat earthquake and the Godhra riots, this compelling story deals with business, passion, religion, and friendship.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

The first book in the Harry Potter series

by J.K. Rowling

About This Book

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (or Sorcerer's Stone in the US) is the first novel in the Harry Potter series. The book follows Harry Potter, a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his 11th birthday. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, makes new friends, learns about magic, and confronts the dark wizard who murdered his parents. This enchanting story has captivated millions of readers worldwide.
